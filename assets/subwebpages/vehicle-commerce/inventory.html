Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase a vehicle?

expand_more
To purchase a vehicle, navigate to the "Buy Now" section, select your preferred car, and proceed with the checkout process.

What payment methods are accepted?

expand_more
We accept credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. Financing options are also available for eligible buyers.

Can I schedule a test drive?

expand_more
Yes, you can schedule a test drive by clicking on the "Schedule Test Drive" button available on the vehicle's details page.

Is there a return policy?

expand_more
Yes, we offer a 7-day return policy for vehicles under specific terms and conditions. Please check our return policy page for details.

What is the warranty on purchased vehicles?

expand_more
All our vehicles come with a standard warranty, ranging from 6 months to 2 years. Extended warranty options are also available.

How do I apply for vehicle financing?

expand_more
You can apply for vehicle financing through the "Finance Options" page. Fill out the application form, and our team will assist you.

Can I trade in my current vehicle?

expand_more
Yes, we accept trade-ins. Use our trade-in calculator to estimate your current vehicle's value and start the trade-in process.

How do I track my order status?

expand_more
Log into your account and navigate to the "My Orders" section to view the real-time status of your vehicle purchase.

What happens after I place an order?

expand_more
Once your order is placed, you will receive a confirmation email. A sales representative will contact you to finalize the details.
