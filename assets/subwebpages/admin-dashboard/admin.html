How GP Margin is Calculated

Formula:

GP Margin = (Revenue - Cost of Goods Sold) / Revenue * 100

For this example, the GP Margin is 20%, meaning that for every dollar of revenue, the business makes a 20% profit after covering the costs of the vehicles sold.
